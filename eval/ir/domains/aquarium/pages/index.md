# Freshwater Planted Aquarium Care

This wiki documents the **Greenwater Method**, an internally-consistent system for running a high-tech, CO2-injected planted freshwater aquarium. Every page assumes the same baseline: a 40 litre reference tank held at 24 °C, pH 6.6, with 30 ppm CO2 and a 7-hour photoperiod.
