# Conditions & Healing

When your Vigor (see [Attributes](attributes.md)) drops, conditions begin to stack.

## Bleeding Out

Reaching **0 Vigor** does not kill you outright; instead you fall and begin **Bleeding Out**. At the start of each of your turns you roll **1d6, and on a 1 or 2 you lose a Death Token**. A character who accumulates **3 Death Tokens dies**. Any healing that restores you above 0 Vigor clears all Death Tokens.

## Common conditions

- **Scorched** — you take **2 damage at the start of each turn** until the fire is doused. Caused by many [Magic System](magic.md) effects.
- **Staggered** — you lose your Move for one exchange.
- **Blinded** — your [Ember Pool](dice-system.md) loses 2 dice on sight-based checks.
- **Cinderlocked** — you have no [Ember Points](attributes.md) and cannot cast; explained on the [Magic System](magic.md) page.

## Recovery

Natural healing restores **Grit + 1 Vigor per full night of rest**. A successful Medicine check (difficulty 2 sparks) during a short rest restores **1d6 Vigor** to one ally, usable once per character per day.

A **full week of downtime restores all Vigor and clears every condition** except those marked permanent. The [Hollow Warden](callings.md) and [Cindermage](callings.md) both gain access to healing spells in the [Spell Compendium](spells.md).
