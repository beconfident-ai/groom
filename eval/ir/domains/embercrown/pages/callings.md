# The Callings

Embercrown has **five Callings**, the game's character classes. Each grants a signature feature at Crownstep 1 (see [Advancement](advancement.md)).

## Ashblade

The frontline warrior. Favored attribute is **Grit**. Signature feature: **Cinderstrike** — once per exchange, when an Ashblade lands a melee hit they deal **+1d6 damage** and ignore the target's armor.

## Cindermage

The fire-channeler. Favored attribute is **Resolve**. A Cindermage begins play with **2 extra Ember Points** beyond the normal total and knows three spells from the [Spell Compendium](spells.md). Their signature feature is **Kindle**, restoring 1 EP whenever they roll a Surge.

## Hollow Warden

The keeper of the wild places. Favored attribute is **Insight**. Signature feature: **Beastspeech** — a Warden may speak with any natural animal and gains a bonded companion drawn from the [Bestiary](bestiary.md).

## Veiltongue

The infiltrator and negotiator. Favored attribute is **Finesse**. Signature feature: **Backstab** — an attack from stealth or against an unaware target deals **double damage**. Veiltongues also reroll all 1s on Larceny checks.

## Emberbound

The oath-sworn defender of the Embercrown. Favored attribute is **Presence**. An Emberbound swears a single Oath at creation; while the Oath is unbroken they gain **+2 Vigor** and may once per session reroll their entire [Ember Pool](dice-system.md). See [Setting & Lore](setting.md) for the Embercrown relic they serve.
