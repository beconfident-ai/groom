# Bestiary

Each entry lists a creature's **Vigor, Defense, and attack pool** (rolled as an [Ember Pool](dice-system.md)). Defense and Vigor follow the same rules as player characters in [Attributes](attributes.md).

## Ashhound

A pack predator of the ash plains. **Vigor 12, Defense 10.** Attacks with a bite pool of **5 dice for 2d6 damage**. Ashhounds hunt in packs of **four to six** and gain **+1 die** to attacks while flanking. They are the bonded companion most often chosen via [Beastspeech](callings.md).

## Cinderwraith

The restless dead of those who burned in the [Sundering](setting.md). **Vigor 18, Defense 12.** Its touch attack rolls **6 dice** and inflicts the **Scorched** condition (see [Conditions & Healing](conditions.md)) on a hit. A Cinderwraith is **immune to fire damage** and can only be harmed by Glyphcraft or physical weapons.

## Glasswyrm

A serpent of fused sand that haunts the Glass Waste. **Vigor 40, Defense 14.** Its bite deals **4d6 damage**, and once per exchange it breathes a cone of molten glass dealing **3d6 fire damage** to everything within 10 metres. The Glasswyrm is a major threat worth **2 bonus Embers** when slain, per [Advancement](advancement.md).

## Hollow Knight

An armored revenant sworn to a broken Oath. **Vigor 30, Defense 13.** Wields a greataxe for **3d6 damage** and may take **two Actions per exchange**. Hollow Knights were once [Emberbound](callings.md) who failed their charge to guard the Embercrown.
