# Magic System

All magic in Embercrown draws on **the Ember**, the smouldering world-soul that survived the Sundering described in [Setting & Lore](setting.md). Casters pull power through their [Resolve](attributes.md).

## Ember Points

Every spell costs **Ember Points (EP)**, your pool equal to **Resolve + 2**. To cast, you spend the spell's EP cost and roll a Channeling [Ember Pool](dice-system.md) of **Resolve + Channeling** against the spell's difficulty. The full list of costs is in the [Spell Compendium](spells.md).

## The three schools

Embercrown magic has **exactly three schools**:

- **Pyromancy** — raw fire, heat, and destruction.
- **Glyphcraft** — wards, sigils, and lasting enchantments, rolled with the Glyphwork skill.
- **Veilcraft** — illusion, shadow, and mind, favored by the [Veiltongue](callings.md).

## Overdraw

When you lack the EP for a spell, you may **Overdraw**: pay the missing EP as **Vigor at a rate of 2 Vigor per 1 EP**. Overdrawing always inflicts the **Scorched** condition on the caster (see [Conditions & Healing](conditions.md)).

## Cinderlock

The moment your EP reaches **0**, you become **Cinderlocked**. While Cinderlocked you cannot cast at all and may only recover EP by resting. You regain **1 EP per hour of rest**, and a full night restores your entire EP pool. The [Cindermage](callings.md) can break Cinderlock mid-fight, because their Kindle feature restores 1 EP whenever they roll a Surge.
