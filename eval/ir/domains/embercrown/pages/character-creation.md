# Character Creation

Building an Embercrown character takes five steps.

## Step 1 — Attributes

All five [attributes](attributes.md) begin at **2**. You receive **4 advancement points** to raise them, where each point raises one attribute by 1. No attribute may exceed **4** at creation.

## Step 2 — Choose a Calling

Pick one of the five [Callings](callings.md): Ashblade, Cindermage, Hollow Warden, Veiltongue, or Emberbound. Your Calling grants a feature and a list of favored skills.

## Step 3 — Skills

There are **twelve skills** in Embercrown: Melee, Marksmanship, Athletics, Stealth, Lore, Survival, Medicine, Insight, Persuasion, Channeling, Glyphwork, and Larceny. You distribute **8 skill ranks** among them, and no single skill may start above **rank 3**. Skills add to your [Ember Pool](dice-system.md).

## Step 4 — Derived values

Calculate Vigor (10 + Grit × 3), Ember Points (Resolve + 2), and Defense (7 + Finesse) as described in [Attributes](attributes.md).

## Step 5 — Starting gear

Every new character begins with **15 ardents**, the silver coin of the [Sundered Reach](setting.md). You also choose one Calling kit and one personal trinket tied to your backstory.

New characters start at **Crownstep 1**; the [Advancement](advancement.md) page covers how you grow from there. A starting character's typical Vigor is **16**, assuming Grit 2.
