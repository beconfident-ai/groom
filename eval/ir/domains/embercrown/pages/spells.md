# Spell Compendium

Each spell lists its **school, EP cost, difficulty (sparks), and effect**. Casting rules are on the [Magic System](magic.md) page; difficulties are read against the [Ember Pool](dice-system.md).

## Pyromancy

- **Ember Dart** — 1 EP, difficulty 2. Hurls a mote of fire dealing **2d6 fire damage** to one target within 20 metres.
- **Wreath of Cinders** — 3 EP, difficulty 3. All enemies within 5 metres take **1d6 damage** and gain the **Scorched** condition (see [Conditions & Healing](conditions.md)).
- **Pyre Lance** — 5 EP, difficulty 4. A beam dealing **5d6 fire damage** in a line up to 30 metres long.

## Glyphcraft

- **Ward Sigil** — 2 EP, difficulty 2. Inscribes a glyph granting one ally **+3 Defense for 3 exchanges**.
- **Binding Rune** — 4 EP, difficulty 3. Roots a target in place for **2 exchanges** unless they beat the caster's Channeling pool.
- **Mending Glyph** — 3 EP, difficulty 2. Restores **2d6 Vigor** to a touched ally; one of the few healing spells available to the [Cindermage](callings.md).

## Veilcraft

- **Cloak of Ash** — 2 EP, difficulty 2. The caster becomes invisible for **1 minute** or until they attack.
- **Whispered Lie** — 1 EP, difficulty 2. Plants a single false memory the target accepts unless their Resolve beats the cast.
- **Hollow Step** — 4 EP, difficulty 3. Teleports the caster up to **15 metres** to any spot they can see, favored by the [Veiltongue](callings.md).
