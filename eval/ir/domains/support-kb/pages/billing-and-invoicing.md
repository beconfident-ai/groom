# Billing & Invoicing

Billing is managed in **Settings → Billing** by any user with the **Owner** or **Billing Admin** role (see [Account & SSO Setup](account-and-sso.md)).

## Cycles and discounts

Self-serve plans bill monthly by default. Switching to **annual billing** gives a **20% discount** — for example, the Team plan is $199/month or $1,910/year. Annual plans are charged upfront and renew automatically on the anniversary date.

## Payment methods

We accept all major credit cards and, on the **Business** and **Enterprise** tiers, payment by ACH bank transfer or wire against a net-30 invoice. Cards are processed by our payment provider; Lumen never stores raw card numbers.

## Invoices and receipts

Every charge generates a PDF invoice emailed to the billing contact and available under **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**. Invoices list the base plan, any event or storage overages, and applicable tax. You can add a **VAT ID or tax note** that appears on every future invoice in the same screen.

## Failed payments

If a card charge fails, Lumen retries **three times over seven days**. During this dunning period the workspace stays fully active. After the third failure the workspace is downgraded to **Free** and data beyond the Free retention window becomes inaccessible until billing is restored.

## Refunds

Monthly plans are **non-refundable**. Annual plans cancelled within **14 days** of purchase are eligible for a full refund; after 14 days annual plans are not refunded but remain active until the term ends. Overage charges are never refundable. See [Plans & Limits](plans-and-limits.md) for overage rates.
