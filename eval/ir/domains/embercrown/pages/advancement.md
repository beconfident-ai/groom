# Advancement

Embercrown characters improve by earning experience called **Embers** and spending a currency called **Glow**.

## Earning Embers

The table awards **Embers** at the end of each session. A typical session grants **3 Embers**: 1 for showing up, 1 for advancing the story, and 1 for a standout moment of roleplay or daring. Defeating a major threat from the [Bestiary](bestiary.md) grants **2 bonus Embers**.

## Spending Glow

Embers are not spent directly. At the end of a session you convert them: **every 5 Embers become 1 Glow**. Glow is the points you actually spend on improvements:

- Raise a skill by one rank: **2 Glow** (see [Character Creation](character-creation.md) for the skill list).
- Raise an [attribute](attributes.md) by one point: **6 Glow** (maximum 6).
- Learn a new spell from the [Spell Compendium](spells.md): **3 Glow**.

## Crownsteps

Character level is measured in **ten Crownsteps**, numbered 1 to 10. You reach a new Crownstep each time you have spent a cumulative **10 Glow**. So Crownstep 2 requires 10 spent Glow, Crownstep 3 requires 20, and so on.

Each Crownstep also grants **+3 maximum Vigor** and, at **Crownsteps 4, 7, and 10**, a new feature for your [Calling](callings.md). A character reaches the legendary **Crownstep 10**, the highest tier, after spending 90 Glow total.
