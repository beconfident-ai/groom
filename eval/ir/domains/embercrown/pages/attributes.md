# Attributes

Every Embercrown character has **five attributes**, each rated from 1 to 6. They feed the [Ember Pool](dice-system.md).

- **Grit** — physical toughness, strength, and endurance.
- **Finesse** — agility, reflexes, and precision.
- **Insight** — perception, memory, and cunning.
- **Resolve** — willpower and the capacity to channel the Ember for [Magic](magic.md).
- **Presence** — force of personality, leadership, and command.

At creation an attribute starts at **2** and may be raised no higher than **4**. The hard ceiling reachable through play is **6**.

## Derived resources

Three resources are calculated from attributes:

- **Vigor** is your hit-point pool, equal to **10 + (Grit × 3)**. A character with Grit 3 has 19 Vigor. Reaching 0 Vigor knocks you out; see [Conditions & Healing](conditions.md).
- **Ember Points (EP)** fuel spells, equal to **Resolve + 2**. A character with Resolve 4 has 6 EP. Spending rules are on the [Magic System](magic.md) page.
- **Momentum** is a shared party pool, not personal. The party begins each session with **3 Momentum** and may bank a maximum of **6**. Momentum is spent to add dice to pools as described in the [Dice System](dice-system.md).

Your **Defense**, the difficulty an enemy must beat to hit you, equals **7 + Finesse** and is detailed in [Combat](combat.md).
