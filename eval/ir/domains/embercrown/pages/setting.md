# Setting & Lore

Embercrown is set in the **Sundered Reach**, a continent of ash plains, glass deserts, and ember-lit cities that survived a cataclysm called the Sundering.

## The Sundering

**Three hundred years ago** the world's central sun, the Great Hearth, shattered. Its fall scorched the land and left behind the **Ember**, a smouldering world-soul from which all [Magic](magic.md) is now drawn. The event is simply called **the Sundering**, and the calendar counts years as **AS (After Sundering)**; the current year is **AS 312**.

## Vael Ardent

The capital and largest surviving city is **Vael Ardent**, the City of Coals, built into the caldera of the fallen Hearth. It is ruled by the **Council of Nine Wicks**. The common coin of the realm is the **ardent**, a silver piece; new characters start with 15 of them per [Character Creation](character-creation.md).

## The Embercrown

The game is named for the **Embercrown**, a relic forged from a single shard of the Great Hearth. Whoever wears it can command the Ember itself, and so it is the most fought-over object in the Reach. The **Emberbound** [Calling](callings.md) swear their Oaths to protect or recover it.

## Notable places

- **The Glass Waste** — a desert of fused sand east of Vael Ardent, home to the Glasswyrm of the [Bestiary](bestiary.md).
- **Hollowmere** — a drowned forest ruled by the [Hollow Wardens](callings.md).
- **The Cinder Road** — the only safe trade route across the Reach.
